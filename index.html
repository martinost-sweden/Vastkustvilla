How to get in

The Yale locks open easily with a tag. To enter the house you need a yellow tag. You will find it by walking between the house and the garage. Behind the garage on the left side (north) is the guest house. Behind the guest house (north-east corner) there is a padlock at the bottom of the drainpipe. Open the cover and enter the code 4992. Then pull down the small black lever and open the compartment. Yellow tag for the house and black tag for the pool house. Welcome!

So kommt ihr rein

Die Yale-Schlösser öffnen sich ganz einfach mit einem Chip. Um ins Haus zu kommen, benötigt ihr einen gelben Chip. Den findet ihr, wenn ihr zwischen Haus und Garage entlanggeht.
